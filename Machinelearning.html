To simplify the notation:

\begin{equation} h(x) = \sum{\theta_{i}x_{i}} = \theta^{T}x \end{equation}

Then we define the cost function:

\begin{equation} J(\theta) = \frac{1}{2}\sum{(h_{\theta}(x^{(i)}) - y^{(i)})^{2}} \end{equation}
